It was a winter night.
It was cold.
Annie has a house in the woods.
She is there with her family.
She has a son.
His name is Alex.
She has a daugther.
Her name is Amber.
The night was dark.
Alex feared the dark.
He drank a glass of milk.
He felt better after that.
The fireplace was warm.
It felt good.
Amber likes snow.
She likes making snowballs.
But it was dark for her to do that.
She wanted to play with his brother.
But it was dangerous in that cold.
So they didn't go out.
The house was comfortable.
So they felt good.
Annie made some food.
Amber and Alex ate it.
Alex wanted to sleep.
But Amber didn't want.
She had a glass of milk too.
After that she wanted to sleep too.
They went to sleep.
